Currently Joy is in his Junior year of Highschool , but recently he has noticed feelings of sadness dawn on him every day. He feels distant from everyone and will feel lonier the more he thinks about his sadness. Not only is he starting to realize he is feeling sad, but he is also missing a bunch of his homeworks in school. He is starting to feel greatly overwhelmed by school.
