Mental Growth. An interactive fiction game about living with mental illnesses.
    This website aims to educate teens on the various mental illnesses people have so that it is easier to communicate with people who have mental illnesses.

    Our goals are to :

  1. Help Teens understand what they are dealing with
  2. Inform those who don't know how to help a person with a mental Illness
  3. Provide teens with a more positive outlook on their mental illness.
